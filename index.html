About Me

~/about.txt

I am a Cloud Backend Engineer at BMW Group, specializing in the integration and orchestration of Automated Driving within BMW Plants on a global scale. My work focuses on transforming factory logistics by enabling vehicles to navigate and operate autonomously to maximize operational efficiency.

As an Integration Specialist, I build the critical infrastructure that orchestrates the interaction between autonomous vehicles and the factory's digital ecosystem. This involves architecting Java-based microservices and AWS Cloud backends that facilitate real-time data exchange between on-premises Linux systems and cloud-based management layers.

With a degree in Autonomous Vehicle Engineering and a deep background in Computer Vision and AI, I bridge the gap between the vehicle's "brain" (perception and planning) and the factory's "nervous system" (cloud and backend infrastructure).
